About Us

Cooking Storm is an exciting and fast-paced time-management game where players step into the shoes of a chef, preparing and serving delicious dishes to hungry customers. From street food stalls to high-end restaurants, each level brings new cooking challenges, from managing ingredients to handling a bustling kitchen. Players can unlock new recipes, upgrade their kitchens, and compete against time to serve customers efficiently. Whether you're a fan of cooking games or love a good challenge, Cooking Storm brings the heat and excitement straight to your screen.
