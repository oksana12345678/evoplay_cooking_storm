How to Play

  • Cook and Serve

    Prepare a wide range of dishes using the ingredients available to you and serve them to customers.

  • Upgrade Your Kitchen

    Improve your kitchen appliances, unlock new recipes, and enhance your cooking abilities.

  • Complete Orders

    Serve customers quickly and accurately to earn rewards and advance to new levels.

  • Challenge Yourself

    Face increasingly difficult challenges as you rise through the ranks, unlocking new restaurants and cuisines.

  • Time Management

    Strategize your cooking process to handle multiple orders and avoid mistakes in a fast-paced kitchen environment.
